🧠 Core Strategy: Why This Team?

This team is built for speed and raw power to clear all gym reruns within a single Riches Charm (60 minutes). The goal is to 1HKO or 2HKO every gym leader with minimal switching.

  • Double Typhlosion: Eruption hits both opponents in double battles. With maximum Special Attack and Speed, it outruns and OHKOs most frail or neutral targets.
  • Choice Specs vs Scarf: Specs Typhlosion provides maximum damage; Scarf Typhlosion ensures speed control against faster threats like Electrode or Accelgor.
  • Blastoise + Archeops: Covers Fire, Rock, and Ground types where Eruption is resisted. Water Spout is a 150 BP move at full HP, and Rock Slide provides spread flinch chance.
  • Torkoal + Drought: Sets up sun to boost Eruption and enables a 1-turn Explosion to bring in Typhlosion safely while keeping sun active.
  • Shedinja: Exploits Wonder Guard to wall specific gyms (Water, Normal, Fighting) and forces the AI into useless moves, giving Blastoise free turns.
⚡ Key principle: Every Pokémon is EV-trained for maximum offense. Speed is prioritized to ensure you always move first. The team uses Choice items to boost damage, and spread moves like Eruption and Rock Slide to clear double battles efficiently.

📦 Item Breakdown

  • Choice Specs (Typhlosion): +50% Special Attack. Eruption becomes a nuke that can OHKO even resistant foes like Dragonite after sun.
  • Choice Scarf (Typhlosion & Blastoise): +50% Speed. Allows you to outrun base 130+ speed mons like Jolteon or Crobat. Blastoise with Scarf outspeeds most gym leads.
  • Wide Lens (Archeops): Increases accuracy of Rock Slide to 99%, ensuring you don't miss crucial flinches or KOs.
  • Heat Rock (Torkoal): Extends sun from 5 to 8 turns, giving Typhlosion multiple Eruption boosts without needing to re-set.
  • Focus Sash (Shedinja): Prevents OHKO from Mold Breaker or weather chip, guaranteeing at least one turn to pivot or status.

⚔️ Move Selection Logic

  • Eruption: The star move. Hits both opponents and scales with HP. With Specs and sun, it's the most efficient spread move in the game.
  • Water Spout: Blastoise's equivalent to Eruption. Hits Water-weak types (Rock, Ground, Fire) for massive damage.
  • Rock Slide: Archeops's spread move. Hits Flying, Bug, and Ice types. Wide Lens ensures reliability.
  • Explosion: Torkoal's suicide move to bring in a sweeper while setting sun. Also deals heavy damage to Sturdy users.
  • Shadow Sneak / X-Scissor: Shedinja's priority and coverage. Shadow Sneak picks off low-HP targets, X-Scissor hits Dark and Psychic types.
  • Utility moves (Swift, Extrasensory, Protect): Filler moves for coverage or to stall turns when needed (e.g., Protect on Shedinja to scout).

🎯 Pokémon Roles

  • Typhlosion (Specs): Main sweeper. Used against Grass, Bug, Steel, Ice, and neutral matchups. Hits like a truck.
  • Typhlosion (Scarf): Speed control sweeper. Used against faster teams (Elesa, Volkner) or when you need to outrun a specific threat.
  • Blastoise: Water-type specialist. Used against Fire, Rock, and Ground gyms. Also serves as a backup special sweeper.
  • Archeops: Physical spread attacker. Used against Flying, Bug, and Ice types. Rock Slide flinch can cheese wins.
  • Torkoal: Sun setter and suicide lead. Used against Dragon gyms (Iris/Clair) to enable Eruption sweeps.
  • Shedinja: Defensive pivot. Used against Water, Normal, and Fighting gyms to wall the AI and force safe switches.
